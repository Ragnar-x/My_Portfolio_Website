About Me

Hi, I’m Tshiamo — a self-taught front-end web developer from South Africa with a growing foundation in Computer Science. I have a passion for turning ideas into digital experiences and thrive on creating clean, functional, and visually engaging websites. I’m passionate about mixing graphic design with UI/UX design, because I enjoy both the creative and problem-solving sides of building digital experiences. I consider myself a lifetime student, always eager to learn and grow. The idea of building algorithms and creating things with code excites me, and I thrive on turning abstract concepts into functional, engaging solutions. For me, combining creativity with art is not just a process — it’s the foundation of how I approach design and development. I look forward to collaborating on projects where I can bring fresh ideas, precision, and creativity to the table.
